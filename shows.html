Fantasiestücke!

This was a show I had hoped to do for a while. Since missing my undergraduate 4th year recital I have been dying to perform solo double bass music at home in St. John's NL. This recital with the incredible Stella Hui on piano, was my love letter to German romantic music. The program included the Tarantella by Simandl, Arpeggione Sonata by Schubert, Légende by Misek, and Fantasy Pieces by Schumann.
